Project Title: Top chocolate brands in UK By Age

Tag: #MakeoverMondayCoursera, 2018 Week 13


Description: 
In this project, I meticulously analyzed and visualized the preferences of individuals across
various age groups in the UK, highlighting the top chocolate brands among those aged 18-24, 35-44, and 65+.
Through insightful data visualization, I provided a comprehensive overview of consumer trends, 
shedding light on the distinct choices within each age bracket.
